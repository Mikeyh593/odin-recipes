Mikey's Famous Eggs

Description

  • 4 Eggs
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Crack eggs in a bowl
  2. Add a dash of milk
  3. Heat pan on low
  4. Add butter to pan and cook until it is bubbling
  5. Scramble eggs in bowl and add to pan
  6. Slowly push eggs to center of pan in circular motion
  7. Remove from heat when eggs are ninety percent cooked
  8. Serve!
